This folder contains replacement files for data which cannot be patched with the "FixBugs" assembly flag.
Their changes are noted below; apply them at your discretion:

_maps/
 - Buzz Bomber.asm
	Fixed its second firing frame being cut-off

 - Sonic.asm
	Realigned Sonic's ducking sprite to be correctly centered

	Realigned two of his walking sprites to accomodate for their graphical changes

artnem/
 - Sega Logo (REVXX).nem
	Removed stray pixels

 - Title Screen Foreground.nem
	Made the bottom line of the 3rd E in "THE HEDGEHOG" match the thickness of the first 2

 - Monitors.nem
 	Changed Eggman's jacket from blue to red with yellow stripes (from Sonic 2)
	Updated Sonic's icon to match the HUD's life icon.
	Added shoe stripe to speed-up monitor

 - Signpost.nem
	Added yellow stripes to Eggman's jacket (from 2013)

 - Spring Vertical.nem
	Fixed green tint on vertical springs by matching them to upright ones

artunc/
 - Sonic.unc
	Added shoe stripes to frames that were missing them (mostly from Sonic 2)

	Fixed some inconsistently drawn pixels in various sprites

	Backported graphical changes introduced in Sonic Clackers, mainly replacing
	the TTS-style shading for his shoes in the walking animation, among other
	small corrections

	Fixed his hand being cut-off in the third part of his walking animation,
	something that occurred when his head was updated after Tokyo Toy Show
	(credit to Chilidog for pointing this out)

	Fixed his muzzle being cut-off in the first and third frames of his
	walking animation. This necessitates using the modified _maps/Sonic.asm
	due to shifting some of the graphics around.

objpos/
 - ghz1.bin
	Removed misplaced Motobug at 0C50,034C

 - ghz2.bin
	Removed misplaced Crabmeat at 10D0,0370

 - ghz3 (REVXX).bin
	Removed misplaced rock at 18D4,0771 and misplaced Buzz Bomber at 21B0,0000

 - syz2.bin
	Removed misplaced spring at 0B10,0ABA

 - lz1 (REVXX).bin
	Removed misplaced spikes at 12D3,0675, signpost is no longer partially buried in the ground

 - lz2.bin
	Moved hidden points closer to the signpost so that their graphics now load correctly,
	signpost is no longer partially buried in the ground

 - slz2.bin
	Added an invisible wall to prevent Sonic from jumping over a certain wall (lifted from
	Sonic Jam, originally ripped by Devon)

 - sbz2.bin
	Replace monitors inside of walls with invisible blocks
	(read '26, 2E Monitors and Power-Ups.asm' for more information)

 - sbz3.bin
	Small platform in hidden path now appears correctly

palette/
 - Title Screen.bin
	Updated the water to match in-game Green Hill Zone's water

 - Cycle - Title Screen Water.bin
	Updated the water to match in-game Green Hill Zone's water,
	a bit redundant, as you can just delete this file and PalCycle_Title
	and call PalCycle_GHZ on the title screen instead.
